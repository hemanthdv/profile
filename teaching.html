Deep Learning, CSC8851 (Graduate)

Spring 2025, Fall 2024, Fall 2022 (CSC8852)
This class includes advanced deep learning topics like:
(i) Unsupervised Learning: Autoregressive Models, Flow Models, Latent Models (VAE), Implicit Models (GANs), Diffusion Models (ii) Semi-supervised Learning, (iii) Self-supervised Learning, (iv) Energy based models, (v) Bayesian neural networks and (vi) Graph Neural Networks.

Spring 2024, Spring 2023

(i) Single and multilayer neural networks, (ii) Training and Optimizing neural networks, (iii) Convolution neural networks, (iv) Object detection and image segmentation, (v) Generative adversarial networks, (vi) Sequence models and (vii) Diffusion models for image generation.

@ASU - Fundamentals of Statistical Learning, CSE 569 (Graduate)

Fall 2019, Fall 2018

Spring 2021, Fall 2019
The introduction to deep learning systems for computer vision includes:
